There are thousands of people and projects all over the world, doing good work, often with similar aims and in similar fields, but they all start from scratch, spend time, energy and resources building the networks and gaining access to the communities and strategic partners that are necessary to make meaningful progress.

Recognizing that impact could be amplified by more strategic efforts and collaborative interventions...

PROBLEM SOLVER CENTRAL was born!

What Is It?

A strategic hub for the global non-profit community. Offering a library of resources from experts in the sector. Connecting people and projects with networks of support. Allowing for collaborative problem-solving on a global scale.

ESSENCE

It allows for people who can offer resources by:

  • Offering skills and support
  • Making introductions to networks of people, communities, projects and more
  • Offer what you'd like anonymously or publicly with the option to have all contact moderated

It offers a way for those who want to make a difference to reach out to those offering legitimate resources in a secure way.

It allows for global collaborative working through the Solutions Hub, where people can post problems that they want collaborators to help on. People can enter the hub, engage in discussions, and work toward solutions. In many instances a smaller group may be chosen from this larger discussion group, to enter a separate 'lounge area' of the hub, to finalise solutions, presentations etc.

Allows for non-profits to gain access to a library of resources from experts in the sector- for example, resources on set-up and governance, fundraising, financial management, development toolkits, monitoring & evaluation tools etc.
